Control 17/08/17

  • When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?
    • When something doesn't work when I fully expect it to. I had something like this yesterday with my sound file not playing after I followed all the right steps. I realised I shouldn't be so triggered and worked through it. Turns out my code was fine, it was just an issue with the mp3 file I was using.

  • How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?
    • This technique looks really good, and if there's one thing to take away from this, it'll be the stop part and just take it in before any reaction. I will use this next time I come across any such triggers.
