Reflection 12/07/17

  • What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?
    • It sounds like EDA is quite a unique and inspiring learning experience and one that allows for more creativity than your typical course.

  • What are your impressions?
    • This course will get you to work together and help each other progress. I like the analogy that everyone will come together like chefs in a kitchen working together to reach the end result.

  • How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?
    • As an introverted person it may be hard for me at first but I know I will be alright once I jump straight into it.

  • Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?
    • My expectations of EDA haven't changed too much from the Day in the Life event, but before that I guess I thought it wouldn't be as involved and team-based.

  • Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?
    • I'm probably somewhere between nervous and excited. I'm not used to this style of learning, I'm hoping I can just step-in and embrace it.
